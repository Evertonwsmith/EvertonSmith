About the Book

Steady love. Uncomfortable realizations. Brutal heartbreak. Finally, peace. This collection of poetry offers a glimpse into a young woman's journey through a painful and necessary chapter in her life. It is deeply personal, albeit relatable. She leaves you lingering on her words for days after reading, and nostalgic for emotions you thought you'd never miss.
